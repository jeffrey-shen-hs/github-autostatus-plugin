Bucket to be prepended to the metrics you are sending (with trailing period). Example:
  • service.jenkins.pipeline-metrics.
Final metrics paths will follow the pattern:
  • {bucket}.{folder}.{subfolder}.{job name}.branch.{branch}.job.status
  • {bucket}.{folder}.{subfolder}.{job name}.branch.{branch}.job.duration
  • {bucket}.{folder}.{subfolder}.{job name}.branch.{branch}.stage.{stage name}.status
  • {bucket}.{folder}.{subfolder}.{job name}.branch.{branch}.stage.{stage name}.duration
